Carne Frita

Description

Carne frita, which translates to "fried meat," is a traditional Puerto Rican dish primarily made from marinated pork chunks that are
deep-fried until crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. Itâ€™s often seasoned with a mix of garlic, oregano, salt, pepper, and
sometimes a bit of vinegar or lime juice to enhance the flavor.

Ready In: 50 minutes

Serves: 6

Ingredients: 6

  • 3 lbs fresh posk shoulder (Cut into 1 inch chucks)
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon adobo seasoning
  • 5 garlic cloves, mashed to a paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano

Directions

  1. Rinse meat add vinegar, garlic, adobo, oregano, and olive oil. Let marinate 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Deep fry at 350 degrees in medium sized batches till done, about 10 - 12 minutes. Drain and serve.

ENJOY!
