Piña Colada

Description

The Piña Colada is a classic tropical cocktail originating from Puerto Rico. It's a delightful blend of rum, coconut cream,
and pineapple juice, creating a sweet and creamy drink that's perfect for sipping on a warm day. The ingredients are blended
with ice to create a smooth and frosty texture.

Ready In: 5 minutes

Serves: 2

Ingredients: 5

  • 4 ounces cream of coconut
  • 4 ounces pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 ounces rum (Bacardi Gold)
  • 1 1/2 ounces coconut rum (Malibu)
  • 2 cups crushed ice

Directions

  1. Mix ingredients in blender until smooth; pour into glasses; garnish with pineapple wedges and maraschino cherries if desired.

ENJOY!
